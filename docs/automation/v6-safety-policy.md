# v6 automation safety policy

## Allowed in Group 27

Read reviewed/trusted local state and propose non-destructive internal review, Follow-Up, Work Pipeline, agenda/task draft or evidence-review intent. Every mutating proposal requires explicit approval and remains unexecuted.

## Blocked in Group 27

External writes, communication sends, calendar changes, financial mutations, destructive changes, arbitrary scripts, plugins, self-approval, background execution, scheduling, startup execution, automatic retries and AI-generated decisions. Displayed risk labels cannot downgrade a blocked action.
