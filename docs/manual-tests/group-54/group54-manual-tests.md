# LifeOS Group 54 manual proof

## Boundary

Group 54 delivers the Full Mobile Work surface for active jobs, client context, communication review, meetings, follow-ups and evidence. It does not send email, post to Teams, alter external calendars or mutate provider systems.

## Manual checks

1. Open Work and confirm Active, Waiting, Blocked and Due soon summaries.
2. Open a work item and confirm client, next action, people, files, evidence and due date.
3. Open communication review and confirm Gmail, Outlook and Teams candidates are normalized and review-first.
4. Open provenance and confirm provider, source kind, freshness, review state and payload-exclusion boundary.
5. Review a candidate using Accept or Defer and confirm no send/post control exists.
6. Open meeting context and capture a fictional follow-up action.
7. Open the evidence checklist and confirm attachment metadata is shown without automatic body download.
8. Turn Wi-Fi off, capture a follow-up and confirm the update is described as queued safely offline.
9. Confirm no imported communication silently overwrites a trusted work item.
10. Run targeted tests, all repository regressions and the Android Release build.
