# LifeOS Group 55 manual proof

## Boundary

Group 55 delivers a conservative Full Mobile Money surface for trusted summaries, invoice/payment status, receipt evidence capture, evidence review, duplicate candidates and privacy. It does not initiate payments, approve accounting, write to accounting systems or delete original evidence.

## Manual checks

1. Open Money and confirm trusted Income, Expenses, Outstanding invoices and Payments received cards.
2. Open Invoices and payments and confirm status, amount, due date and linked proof.
3. Open Receipt evidence and create a sanitized preview.
4. Categorize/link evidence only through an explicit action.
5. Turn Wi-Fi off and queue evidence; confirm the offline queued message.
6. Open Evidence review and confirm the duplicate candidate remains review-only.
7. Confirm original evidence remains present beside the duplicate candidate.
8. Open Privacy mode and confirm amounts/client details are obscured.
9. Confirm no payment initiation or accounting-provider write exists.
10. Run targeted tests, full regressions and Android Release build.
