# LifeOS Group 56 manual proof

## Boundary

Group 56 delivers Full Mobile Life and Projects surfaces. Life views minimize personal, family and health detail. Projects expose milestones, next actions, progress and evidence without silently changing project state or inventing proof.

## Manual checks

1. Open More > Life and confirm Home, Family, Health and Personal area cards.
2. Open Routines and explicitly complete one routine.
3. Open Reminders and explicitly defer one reminder.
4. Open minimized-detail proof and confirm private life details are hidden.
5. Open Projects and confirm Active, Waiting, Blocked and Parked summaries.
6. Open LifeOS Full Mobile project detail and confirm milestone, next action, progress and evidence.
7. Explicitly park or resume a project and confirm the visible state change.
8. Confirm no diagnosis, treatment recommendation, silent project-state mutation or automatic evidence generation exists.
9. Confirm all data is fictional or sanitized.
10. Run targeted tests, all repository regressions and Android Release build.
