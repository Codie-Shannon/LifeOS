# Group 61 — Career opportunity and application pipeline

## Boundaries

- No autonomous application submission.
- No recruiter or employer messaging.
- No external profile mutation.
- No invented qualifications, experience, references, salary history or answers.
- Imported and duplicate opportunities remain review-first.
- Desktop Career Studio remains inside the Career workspace.
- Full Mobile uses explicit local or queued actions only.

## Manual proof

1. Open Career → Career Studio and verify the opportunity pipeline.
2. Verify opportunity detail shows requirements, source, reviewed fit and linked evidence.
3. Verify imported and duplicate candidates remain awaiting review.
4. Verify application checklist and readiness are explicit.
5. Verify timeline, follow-up and interview context are visible.
6. Open Full Mobile Career dashboard and verify closing-soon and waiting-on states.
7. Open application detail and verify explicit offline-safe actions.
