# LifeOS v1.5 Release Notes

## Release

**Version:** v1.5  
**Screenshot group:** Group 01 — Evidence + Relationships  
**Build lane:** Local-first WPF desktop foundation  
**Status:** Screenshot group captured after v1.4 Evidence Vault and v1.5 Relationship Radar were visible in the app.

## What v1.5 adds

v1.5 extends LifeOS from evidence tracking into relationship-state tracking.

The goal is not to connect to real inboxes or expose private client details yet. The goal is to prove the local model:

- relationship profiles
- waiting-on state
- follow-up pressure
- do-not-chase protection
- linked-work behaviour
- fictional/demo-safe relationship records

## Group 01 scope

Group 01 covers:

1. **Command Centre**
   - shows the main operating dashboard
   - keeps pressure, work, money, follow-ups, and proof visible
   - includes navigation to Evidence Vault and Relationship Radar

2. **Evidence Vault**
   - local-first evidence/proof workspace
   - captures proof metadata before OCR, mobile capture, cloud sync, or integrations
   - supports the wider LifeOS proof trail direction

3. **Relationship Radar**
   - local relationship-state workspace
   - tracks open relationship profiles
   - separates waiting-on-them from waiting-on-me
   - surfaces follow-up due pressure
   - protects do-not-chase relationships

## Privacy rule

Screenshots and demo data must stay fictional.

Do not include:

- real company names
- real client names
- real emails
- real phone numbers
- bank/payment details
- tenant/app IDs
- private URLs
- secrets

Allowed examples include fictional placeholders such as:

- Workshop Proof Project
- Client Portal Cleanup
- Door Invoice OCR Proof
- Cloud Admin Follow-up
- Portfolio Review Lead
- Family Archive Project
- Laptop Repair
- Phone Repair
- Vehicle Repair
