## What changed

v1.7 connects paid work, expected money, and proof readiness into one local-first operating layer.

It adds:

- Paid Work / Money / Proof core summary model
- proof-gated money-at-risk calculation
- admin-action count for timesheets, invoices, proof, and expected payments
- copy-ready client/admin update text
- fictional demo data for paid-work screenshots
- Command Centre paid-work risk signals
- Paid Work Centre v1.7 workspace
- Money Timeline proof-gated money panel

## Operating rule

Paid work is not real control until the work session, proof, timesheet, invoice, payment, and safe-money state are visible together.

Expected money is not safe money until paid.

## Privacy rule

The included demo data is fictional. Do not capture real company names, real people, emails, phone numbers, payment details, tenant IDs, private URLs, or secrets.
