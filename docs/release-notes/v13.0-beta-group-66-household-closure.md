# LifeOS v13 Group 66 - Shared Household Workflows and v13 Closure

Group 66 closes the v13 Household and Grocery release lane.

## Added

- Shared household routines with due, due-soon, overdue, completed and deferred states.
- Explicit household assignments with proposed, accepted, deferred, declined and completed states.
- Replenishment review that connects routine checks, inventory gaps and meal needs without mutating grocery lists.
- Household receipt candidates that can suggest Document and Money targets while staying review-first.
- Planned-vs-actual household spending review.
- v13 closure checks for Desktop proof, Mobile proof, tests and screenshot evidence.
- Desktop pages for household routines, replenishment review, receipts/spending and v13 closure.
- Full Mobile household routine and receipt/spending review screens.
- Core tests for routines, assignments, receipt linkage, spend overage and closure blocking.

## Boundaries

- Household assignments do not silently assign work.
- Routine completion does not silently change inventory, grocery or money state.
- Receipt candidates do not mutate Documents or Money automatically.
- Spend reviews do not become financial advice or payment initiation.
- Replenishment review does not order, pay or mutate external carts.
- v13 closure remains blocked until screenshots and validation are captured.
