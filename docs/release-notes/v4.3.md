# LifeOS v4.3 Release Notes

## Release

**LifeOS Desktop v4.3**  
**Group 13:** Money Profile / Hidden Deductions / Safe-to-Spend

## Summary

v4.3 strengthens LifeOS money logic before integrations.

It turns the older money-pressure idea into a clearer money profile:

- visible balance
- confirmed cash
- known weekly obligations
- hidden deductions
- reserves
- buffers
- expected money excluded from safe money
- safe-to-spend before and after hidden deductions
- final safe-to-spend
- confidence gates

## Why this matters

The LifeOS money rule is:

> Expected money is not safe money until paid.

v4.3 makes that rule visible in the app and adds the second important rule:

> Safe-to-spend is not just the bank balance. It is confirmed cash minus obligations, hidden deductions, reserves, buffers, and review gates.

## Boundary

v4.3 remains local/manual.

It does not connect real bank feeds, payslips, IRD/tax portals, accounting systems, open banking, email, OCR automation, OAuth, AI actions, the companion app, or the major workspace redesign.
