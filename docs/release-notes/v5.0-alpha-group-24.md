# v5.0.0-alpha.4 — Group 24 Email Radar foundation

Group 24 formally aligns LifeOS to `v5.0.0-alpha.4` and adds a provider-neutral, local/imported Email Radar foundation.

Implemented:
- central MSBuild version metadata through `Directory.Build.props`
- app-visible ProductVersion constants validated by tests
- local JSON/CSV communication evidence preview and explicit confirmation
- inert text normalization, malformed-row skipping, provenance, audit, and duplicate suspicion
- user-guided Email Radar profiles
- deterministic, explained candidate matching
- explicit confirm/reject review
- confirmed-only communication timeline
- review-first waiting-on/follow-up suggestions

Safety boundary:
- no Gmail, Outlook, Graph, IMAP, POP3, mailbox scanning, sending, mutation, background refresh, AI interpretation, automatic Follow-Up creation, or automatic Work Pipeline mutation
- imported evidence remains untrusted until explicit review
- acceptance and operational handoff remain separate

Group 25 has not started.
